---
title: Gateway is up, but the platform loop is still broken
summary: Hermes responds in the CLI, yet Telegram, Discord, or another platform never completes the round trip.
readingTime: 6 min
---

## What this usually means

The base runtime is probably healthy. The failure is more likely in the platform-facing routing layer.

## First recovery loop

Run the narrowest comparison possible:

1. confirm Hermes still responds in the CLI
2. restart `hermes gateway`
3. send one clean platform message
4. compare the send time with the gateway log window

If the CLI works and the platform does not, keep your attention on routing and platform state.

## What to verify next

Check these in order:

- the gateway is reading the expected environment
- the platform callback or webhook is current
- the destination account or route is correct

Do not redesign the workflow until those are clear.

## If the issue persists

Reduce the test surface to one platform, one account, one message, and one expected reply. A broader test only adds noise.
